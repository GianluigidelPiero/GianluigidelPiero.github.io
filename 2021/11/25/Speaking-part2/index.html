attend family gatherings

There’s a tradition in my family that every member is supposed to attend family gatherings on a festival or wedding.

large-scale meeting. I can see many relatives that are not familiar with, because many of them live in other cities.

It’s not only a method of commemorating traditions but also a chance to strengthen family bonds.

Personally I don’t like family gatherings, but I have to do it becasue I will be considered as a rude person if I don’t accept their invitations. They might think I don’t respect them enough. Being isolated by family members could be a really serious problem.

The gatherings are time-consuming because drawbacks overweigh benefits.

For me, I can hardly find someone of my age. Most people in the gatherings are over 40, whom don’t share a common topic with. They drink, brag and gamble on the tables, talking about something I realy don’t care. It’s really tolerating and frustrating.

What’s more, some drunk sometimes engage in fights, so I don’t really think it’s healthy to family relationships.

I’d rather stay at home, playing video games and listening to music, much better than idling there.

lord of rings

The Lord of the Rings is an epic high-fantasy novel written by English author Tolkien.

Set in Middle-earth, intended to be Earth at some distant time in the past, the story began as a sequel(续集) to Tolkien’s 1937 children’s book The Hobbit, but eventually developed into a much larger work.

It was a birthday gift from my father when I was 10. This series includes three books in total, the fellowship of the ring, the two towers and the return of king. I immediately fell into love with the book after I finished the first chapter.

The story tells of the Dark Lord Sauron, who seeks the One Ring, which contains part of his soul, in order to return to power.

The fate of Middle-earth is in great danger so the Fellowship of the Ring is formed to begin their journey to Mount Doom in the land of Mordor, the only place where the Ring can be destroyed.

I’m really into the book.

The first reason is that it depicts the beautiful scenery of middle-earth. I’m really fascinated by this magical land and all kinds of creatures living on it.

The second reason is that the story is about courage, sacrifice and friendship. It can educate kids what’s right and what’s wrong, which is important for kids.

If you wanna go to the cafe, you have to walk along a path surrounded with trees and flowers. After a three minutes’ walk, you can see it. Actually, it’s quite hard to find and I bet many students don’t even know its existence.

Alibaba is a renowed international corporation specializing in e-commerce, IT technology, retailing and financial service.

Alibaba was founded in 2003 by jack ma and his friends and went to public in 2007.

The majority of employees are programmers. They develop softwares, design algorithms and make sure operations go well.

Other positions are about finance, sales, management and so on.

I major in computer science so it’s my dream to work for a big IT company like Alibaba in the future.

In the meanwhile, Alibaba greatly change people’s life. In the past, people had to go outside to buy items but thanks to Alibaba, now we can buy things online and it’s much cheaper.

Jack ma is very unique businessman and he can convey a strong force of personality when he gives speech.

Jack ma is also a great leader and pioneer. Not ony he konws how to start a business from nothing but also knows how to run a giant company. Ten years ago, he was the first person in china who came up with the concept of e-commerce, which greatly benefited IT industry in china.

I’m keen on football, especially free kick. I’m fascinated by free kick and I really want to master it because it’s cool. I really admire footballers who excel in free kicks.

flexibility of style. various way to take a free kick, some prefer getting the ball around the wall, some prefer ground ball. You will never guess how they use this opportunity until the ball is kicked out.

I started to practice it two years ago with my friend. We found some obstacles to stimulate the wall and one of us played as goalkeeper. We agreed to switch positions every 5 turns.

trained for half a day but little progress was made. I found it difficult to go over and around the obstacles.

But if I increase my force, it could be over the bar.

My friend told me that I need to adjust the spot I kick the ball to make the ball drop sharply after it goes over the wall.

vacuum

It’s produced by Xiaomi, which is one of the biggest manufacturer of consumer electronics and household items in China.

We bought this item two years ago, just before we moved to our new apartment, which is located near Yellow River.

I spent about 500 RMB on this item in taobao during sales promotion in holiday.

Actually, I didn’t expect it can be that expensive because I hadn’t bought a vacuum before. It was always my parents who bought stuff like that.

We use this item quite frequently. As long as I stay at home, I vacuum the carpets and floor in my room every day. Sometimes I volunteer to clean my parents’ room and living room.

Vacuum is an essential item in our home because our life will become a mess without it.

Just as I said before, I lived near Yellow River.

The river brings lots of sediment that mainly comes from loess plateau in the middle reaches of the river.

So when it’s windy and dry, the dust will be carried by wind and spread to every corner of my neighborhood.

It’s really annoying and frustrating.

So I have to use my vacuum to clean my apartment every day.

I can say that almost every family in my neighborhood owns a vaccum. Because we really can’t live without it.

As a matter of fact, I think it’s price is too expensive. It should be an everyday good that every one can afford.

Especially in my city, where almost every family is in great need of vacuum due to weather condition.

I guess it’s because inflation is too high but in the meanwhile our average salary doesn’t go up correspondingly.

Qingdao, which is a costal city in eastern shandong, about 300 km from where I live.

Qingdao is a very beautiful city with mild cliamte and amazing natural sceneries. Almost no disaster has ever happened in qingdao and social order is very stable so visitors don’t need to worry about theft. It’s very safe and low-paced city, very suitable for taking a holiday.

great grandpa

My great grandfather is the person I admire the most in my family. All of us are proud of him.

He was born and raised in an extremely poor family.

He was a vetern, who fought in the second-sino japanese war during 1940s.

He decided to join the army when his hometown was occupied by japanese.

It was a really tough decision because at that time he had been married and got six kids, including my grandfather.

He was the only one in his family who earned money and his family couldn’t live without him.

But he decided to say goodbye to his family and joined army to fight with invaders, despite all his neighbors critisized him for abandoning his family.

He distinguished himself during wars and had been injured a few times. He saw many of his friends dying right in front of him, including his own brother, but he managed to survive.

After the war, he returned home and was recognised as a hero by local people.

When I was a kid, I really liked listening to his legendary stories while sitting around him.

I’m proud of him because he’s a patriot who is willing to sacrifice himself for others.

His experience always reminds me of the importance of peace and the cruelty of wars.

Tangram

Tangram, which means seven boards of skill. It’s kind of dissection puzzle consisting of seven flat polygons. The objective is to replicate a pattern generally found in the puzzle book using all seven pieces without overlap.

According to the legend, it was invented in china around 18th and then carried to America and Europe by trading ships.

Now it has become one of the most widely recognised dissection puzzles in the world and has been used for multipy purpose, such as amusement and eduaction.

It was given to me as a birthday gift by my dad when I was six years old. My father thought it can make children smarter and develop manual ability.

At first, I thought it might be a little hard. It took me a few minutes to figure out the rule and the structure of the puzzle.

Then I started to use my imagination and I successfully got many kinds of patterns, like fish, turtle, candle. But I spent many hours on figuring the rest with the help of a reference book.

I felt really excited about it and it gave me a sense of achievement.

drug company

relocation of a drug company

It has no social responsibility and morality.

I think my country should make more regulations on drug companies. I always have lots of respect for drug companies because their products have saved millions of people’s lives. But the damage they have caused to our environment is evident.

I think I have much to say about it because there is a major drug company located near my home.

The air always smells stinking and the nearby river becomes dark and dirty.

I think drug companies need to allocate more funds on sewage disposal system and waste gas treatment system.

Government is supposed to tighten enforcement of environmental protection standards and raise the amount of tax and fine. What’s more, drug factories should not be allowed to locate near residential areas to prevent residents from incidents, like explosion and gas leaking.

The law will benefit the public and I think it will be popular. The law can ensure residents’ health and sustainable development of society.

Although government may worry about the tax revenue becasue of the reduction of profits. But I don’t think it’s a big deal. If the environment becomes better, there will be more people decide to live and work here, which will raise local tax revenue. What’s more, beautiful natural scenery will attract more visitors, which can boost tourism industry.

areca catechu

I read an article about areca catechu on the internet recently. It was about the harm of areca catechu and was published on a health column.

Actually, I just came across it at first but it caught my eye and left me a deep impression after finishing it.

Areca catechu is a species of palm , which grows in Southern china, mainly in yunnan, guangxi and hainan.

In china, especially in southern china, many people like chewing areca catechu during work time to release pressure.

However, this article gave a warning of its potential bad effects on health.

It said areca catechu can increase the risk of developing mouth cancer, because it contains detrimental chemicals that can harm mouth mucosa. So over time, they are more likely to be troubled by dental ulcer, which will finally develop to mouth cancer.

What’s more, chewing areca catechu can make people look ugly because the nut is so hard that it requires lots of energy to chew it, which will result in the deformation of faces.

After reading the article, I felt really worried, because personally I knew lots of friends and family members who enjoy chewing areca catechu.

So I re-posted the article to them and I hope they can read it and stop chewing areca catechu.

pottery

In acient times, my city was famous for producing all sorts of pottery, and they were sold to every corner of the country, some of them were even seen in europe by trading ships.

So pottery is an important part of my city’s history. In my secondary school, pottery lesson is a compulsory lesson for every student, in order to let students experience the procedure of pottery making and cultivate manual ability.

Before we started, my teacher handed out all the necessary materials, including clay, gloves and water, gravers and other tools.

At first, I decided to make a tiger-shape one, but I gave up after a few attempts because it was complex.

So I changed my mind and made a human face shape pottery. It was quite easy because I just need to fashion a pair of ears, a pair of eyes, a nose and a mouth. That was all I could do in consideration of my ability.

Then it was fired in a stove. After a few hours, I got my work. The pottery I made was not beautiful but I was really satisfied with it. By making a pottery, I learned a lot practical skills.
